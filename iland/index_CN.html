iland

XR心理疗愈新体验

产品介绍

Based on the current decline in the psychological state of the younger generation and the increasing risk of mental health issues, combined with the lack of talent resources in the domestic mental health sector and the public's relative neglect of mental health issues, as well as the absence of relevant applications for psychological therapy and stress relief in the country, and with the development of AR technology, which has become relatively mature for mobile phone applications, but mostly in the toB model and primarily for product promotion. Progress in other application areas has been slow. Considering the current market background and technological aspects, we have developed a mobile AR interactive software for psychological healing and stress relief — **iland**.

受众群体： 16-35学生/职场人士、以女性群体为主
内容设计：  山川自然    古典建筑    传统文化
主体功能：   箱庭    正念    心理测试    
价值主张：   为用户提供心理疗愈、心理状态测试、缓解情绪压力    

产品宣传

借助VR/AR技术，结合传统的正念及箱庭心理疗愈手段，打造一款多平台、沉浸式、线上化的交互应用

视频展示产品正念及箱庭功能效果及界面设计与交互设计演示
